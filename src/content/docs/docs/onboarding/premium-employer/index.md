The Premium Employer status entails different products that enhance your visibility and branding on FashionUnited.

## Organizational News Membership

Certain news articles on FashionUnited are only accessible when a visitor has a paid subscription or is linked to a company/brand which has an Organizational News Membership.

When news access is included in your partnership with FashionUnited, you can log in with your company email address to access the paywalled news articles:

- By signing in via the homepage of FashionUnited (upper right corner)
- Or by signing in via an executive news branded article

:::note
You need to use your company email address, since access is linked to email domains.
:::

If you are not able to access the executive news, contact [jobs@fashionunited.com](mailto:jobs@fashionunited.com).

## Header Image

An Employer Page Premium has its own banner image, brand logo, company description and job overview.

For the banner (shown on top of the page), think of showing part of your collection or a campaign image which gives potential candidates a good first impression.

## Job Template

As a Premium Employer you can receive your own job template. Provide the following materials:

- **Logo** — Your logo as SVG (or PNG) file
- **1–3 images** — Landscape format, 970 px wide and high resolution. All images need to have the same exact dimensions.
- **1 video link** — Public video on YouTube or Vimeo

Images and video can easily be updated (per season for example). Share new materials with [jobs@fashionunited.com](mailto:jobs@fashionunited.com) and all active jobs will be updated.

## Employer Branding Page

As a Premium Employer, an Employer Branding Page can be part of your package. The Employer Branding Page is a customised company page that contains your own banner image, brand logo, company description, and next to the job overview one or two tabs with brand content and news.

Additionally:

- Jobs appear with a **pink highlight** next to them in the job board
- You have an **Employer card** visible on the overview page and underneath each job

Our team will put together the page for you using the provided material.

### What you can send us

- **1 header image**
- **Content for the "about" and "working at" tabs** — These can be texts (via email or Word files), images, and video links. You can also point us to existing pages on your site.
  - **About** — Information about your brand/company (history, values for example)
  - **Working at** — What is it like working at your brand/company (perks and benefits, department information, employee testimonials for example)
- **News** — This tab will automatically contain (positive) news published on FashionUnited

### Guidelines

- **Less is more** — Keep the content simple and see it as a first glimpse candidates have of you as employer, not a replacement or copy of your own website
- The layout of the tabs is set up **mobile first** — meaning simple layouts with no custom font or customised text and image placement
- **No external links** are allowed on the branding page
