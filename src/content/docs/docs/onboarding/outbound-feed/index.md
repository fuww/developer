---
title: Job Feed (FashionUnited to Client)
description: "How to receive a job feed from FashionUnited to display on your own website."
---

We can provide you with an XML URL which contains your jobs currently published on FashionUnited. You then set up the feed connection on your end and can create your own mapping rules — for example, only showing certain positions on your website by filtering on categories, countries or cities.

## How it works

Via the Dashboard you are able to manage the jobs (publish, unpublish, repost and edit). FashionUnited serves as "the source" — the connection between our job board and your website. In this setup, FashionUnited acts as your ATS.

## Setup

The setup of the feed is something you need to do yourself. We are not able to support you with this on a technical level, since all the work needs to happen on your end.

The Customer Service team ([jobs@fashionunited.com](mailto:jobs@fashionunited.com)) can provide you with the job XML URL containing the job content.

## Managing your jobs

Once set up, you manage your jobs through the [FashionUnited Dashboard](https://dashboard.fashionunited.com/):

- **Publish** — Make a job visible on your site and FashionUnited
- **Unpublish** — Remove a job from public view
- **Repost** — Re-publish an expired job
- **Edit** — Update job details
